About Me

A little about me,


In 2002 when I was 14 I bought Elizabeth Castro's HTML for the World Wide Web. Armed with that book, Microsoft Notepad, and Internet Explorer I set out to create my first website. Fast forward to now and the internet has changed a lot it is a more complex and so have the tools we use but my passion for the web and creating things for it has only grown from strength to strength.


The aphorism "Less is more" often attributed to Mies van der Rohe is very mush something I subscribe to. Simply put what is the minimum design required to meet the needs of the end user which still gets the message we are trying to tell them to them. Rather than looking at what looks cool or is trendy at the moment I focus on what will best meet the needs of my clients and the end user.

The way I like to work,


Research

The initial stage of any of my project begins with research. This consists of an initial meeting where we ensure we have clear goals, a desired outcome (How to measure success) and lastly ensure that we are on the same page we leave nothing to chance.

Design

Once we have outlined everything in phase one we move to the design phase. This is where I create a visual expression of everything we agreed upon and from your input we refine this expression until we have the best possible solution.

Develop

Once we have agreed upon the design of the solution we move to the development stage. this is where you get to kick back and relax while I develop the solution into the final product. A fully compliant website in the latest web standards I your customers will love.

Launch

Once we have passed the final review we move to the launch phase. finally we get to see our hard work pay off. The roll out will bring new customers and new opportunities. Full support and training will be supplied so you are not on your own once the project out the door.
