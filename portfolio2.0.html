Mobile-Friendly

The web has changed and so has it's users more users than ever are connecting online with mobile devices as Google Announced in May 2015 more searches take place on mobile devices than on computers in 10 countries including the US and Japan. This mean responsive web design is the new standard for modern web design. It's imperative your site works on all devices or risk being left behind or worse penalised in google natural search. On April 21st 2015 Google rolled out their Mobile-Friendly Update meaning sites which are responsive will appear higher on mobile search results than those which are not. I am well versed in all the lastest techniques in terms of resonsive design and will ensure the solution I design for your project will work perfect on every device.

Search Engine Optimisation

I start forming an SEO strategy for a website even before a line of code is written this bottom up approach ensures nothing is left to chance. This strategy is pivotal to all steps of project and culminates with a final pass with various software to ensure your best foot forward prior to lunch. I can provide training on how to use Google Analytics or I can even take care of it for you and send you automated monthly reports.

Content Management Systems

A content management system (CMS) gives the power back to you the site owner to edit and manage to content on your site. A CMS is ideal for sites which contain events, blogs, news and announcements. SEO Friendly in nature help to grow your audience.
